About Green Journey Advisor

Welcome to Green Journey Advisor, your trusted partner for sustainable travel across the UK. Our mission is to empower travelers to make eco-conscious choices without compromising on convenience or cost.

Founded in 2025, we recognized the growing need for accessible and transparent information regarding the environmental impact of travel. We believe that every journey can be a step towards a greener future, and our platform is designed to make that choice easy and rewarding.

We are committed to providing accurate CO2 emission data, highlighting cost-effective green alternatives, and continuously expanding our network of sustainable transport providers. Join us in making a difference, one journey at a time.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for sustainable travel planning, inspiring millions to choose environmentally friendly journeys and contribute to a healthier planet.

Our Values

  • Sustainability at Core
  • Transparency & Accuracy
  • User-Centric Design
  • Innovation & Growth
